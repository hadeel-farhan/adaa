About the project:

King Hussein Business Park Administration (KHBP) Building achieved EDGE Green Building Certification with impressive efficiency results. EDGE (Excellence in Design for Greater Efficiencies) is a Green Building Certification System developed by IFC, a member of the World Bank Group. EDGE is an important benchmark for green building and a system adopted in more than 140 countries around the world, focusing on identifying the most effective options in terms of cost to design green buildings suitable for the local climate in terms of energy consumption and water and the use of environmentally friendly building materials.

For the KHBP Green Building Certification project, efficiency measures in the existing building were identified, upgraded and documented including the high thermal performance of the building envelope, the efficient indoor lighting, and energy efficient air conditioning systems as well as water efficiency in fixtures, as well as the reuse of construction building materials.

The KHBP project achieved a 47% savings in energy consumption and 44% in water consumption.
